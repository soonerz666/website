The Plunge

The Plunge was a storytelling event held in the backyards of Canberra / Ngunnawal & Ngambri country.

The Plunge kicked off in 2020 and ran until 2023. The idea was simple: bring casual, affordable arts events to young Canberrans, welcoming both new performers and listeners.
